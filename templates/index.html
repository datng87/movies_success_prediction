Can you predict how good a movie will be? Yes you can!

Our movie predictor tool will do that for you. Before you give that actor an offer he can’t refuse, let’s see first if he can attract success. But also see what other indicators can help determine how good/bad your movie will be. Otherwise, it might be Houston, we have a problem.

Turn your box office bomb to a blockbuster with these key features.

Movie year – When should you release it?
Movie length - How long should your movie be for success?
Number of languages - Is it worth translating your movie to other languages?
Budget - The bigger the better or more money more problems?
Has prequel? - Go with an original or the proven success?
Movie released month - Summer blockbuster or winter sleeper hit?
Top actor names - Who's your star billing? Is it a one man show or an ensemble cast?
Rated - Who is your target audience?
Genre – Drama, Comedy or Adventure? Why not have all? Or even a Doco?
