Context: Crimes are reported by five geographical levels in Victoria. They are grouped into 6 separate 'Offence Divisions' with each of these divisions being broken down into Subdivisions and then further into Subgroups.

The chart above provides an overview of the number of incidents by Offence Division from March to March over 4 years. The information can be viewed as a total by selecting 'All' or filtered by any Local Government Area (LGA).

Property and deception offences account for the majority of incidences across all years. They increased in 2020 and have tapered off over the past two years. The number of reported 'crimes against the person' increased slightly in 2020.

The 'Offending 2022' tab at the top right leads to a more granular view of the types and volumes of Subdivisions and Subgroups for each Offence Division for 2022.
